Why Spore Play?

Your kit is carefully nursed from spore to fully colonised and ready to fruit. Only fit and thriving fungi will reach you.

Home lab quality

Every bag is prepared, inoculated, and incubated by Rachel in a sterile home lab — Care and quality control over each kit, no bulk shortcuts.

Natural substrate

I use organic rye grain for your fungi's first meal. It then moves on to a blend of hardwood/straw, soy hulls, and gypsum — the same recipe as the best commercial operations.

Low waste

The substrate makes excellent garden compost after your final harvest. Organic waste produced in kit preparation is added to a local community composting system.

Ready to fruit

Your kit arrives fully colonised. Cut, mist, and watch pins appear within days — no setup, no sterilisation, no specialist kit required.
